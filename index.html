About This Website

This site explains fake news and common social engineering scams, how they work, and how to avoid them.

Examples of Scams

Phishing Email

An email claiming urgent action is required to save your account.

Fake Giveaway

A social post promising free prizes if you enter personal information.

Sensational News

A headline using emotional language to provoke panic or clicks.

Protection Tips

  • Think before clicking links
  • Verify information from official sources
  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
